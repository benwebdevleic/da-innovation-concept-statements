Informed Graduate Recruitment Intelligence Dashboards (INGRID)

The world of graduate recruitment is complex and fast changing. To achieve employment goals, employers must be happy to adapt their strategies in an efficient and evidence-based way. Data plays a key part in informing this change but without centrally sourced data, insights and analysis, key insights may be missed.

INGRID is a platform that allows easy consolidation of relevant graduate recruitment data to reduce the time it takes to go from data to insights.

Our database contains specifically selected, cleansed and merged data from a variety of sources - strongly based around HESA's data - which is collected directly from HE providers and graduates.

INGRID empowers users to analyze, visualize and create dashboards using the pre derived database as well as giving users access to pre derived insights and dashboards.

INGRID helps to inform your graduate recruitment strategy using evidence-based data and insights.
