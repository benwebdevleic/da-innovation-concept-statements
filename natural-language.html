Natural Language

The world of graduate recruitment is complex and fast changing. To achieve employment goals, employers must be happy to adapt their strategies in an efficient and evidence-based way.

Data plays a key part in informing this change but without time and expertise to source and analyse the data, valuable insights might be missed.

Natural language brings the power of data into all employees hands.

Insights can be unlocked by users asking simple questions about any given dataset and the software will generate relevant visualisations.

Our database contains specifically selected, cleansed and merged data from a variety of sources - strongly based around HESA's data - which is collected directly from HE providers and graduates. This natural language approach will help to empower experienced analysts and newcomers with little to no experience in the data world.
